OA CEREMONIES EVALUATOR TRAINING

Welcome to the Online OA Ceremonies Evaluator Training

The purpose of this site is to serve as an online resource where advisers and experienced youth can learn and develop the skills necessary to serve as an evaluator for OA induction ceremonies.

1. Watch the training presentations available on the Evaluator Training page, and familiarize yourself with the provided resources.

2. Watch whole ceremonies on the Practice Evaluations page and complete an evaluation form with skill ratings and feedback for each video.

3. Compare your ratings and feedback to the sample evaluation forms, completed by experienced evaluators.
