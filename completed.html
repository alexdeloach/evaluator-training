Practice Evaluations - Completed Evaluations

Click the button below to find and download the corresponding comments files for the video you just watched. For each ceremony there will be a copy of the text with the memorization and movement errors, a full copy of the notes written by one evaluator during an evaluation, and a document with the ratings and summarized feedback from multiple evaluators.

NOTE: These are the notes written down during a ceremony, not the exact words spoken to the team during the feedback session. During the ceremony you will not have enough time to write notes exactly as you would say them to the team, so try to write down notes that are clear enough to help you remember and give the team some insight for later review but quick enough so you don't spend all your time writing notes. The proper way to deliver verbal feedback to ceremony teams is detailed in Training Module 3: Giving Feedback.
